Bike Ottawa Interactive Maps

Stress Map

Cycling stress map. Shows level of traffic stress (LTS) for every road in the city based on OpenStreetMap and LTS Analyzer model.

Routing

Cycling routing app based on OSRM and LTS map. You can choose your acceptable level of traffic stress for the route you are planning.

Isochrones

Pick any point in the city and see how far you can bike with a user-defined acceptable level of traffic stress.

Collisions

2014-2016 collision map for Ottawa involving pedestrians, cyclists and drivers. Data from City of Ottawa collision open data.
