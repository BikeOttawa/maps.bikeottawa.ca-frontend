OttBike Maps

The following maps are based on Cyclng Level of Traffic Stress project and City of Ottawa collision open data.

Cycling LTS Map

Stress map for Ottawa. Shows level of stress for every road in the city based on OpenStreetMap and LTS Analyzer.

OttBike Routing

Cycling routing app based on OSRM and LTS map. You can select which level of stress is acceptable for the route you are planning.

Isochrones

Pick any point in the city and see how far you can bike with acceptable level of stress.

Collisions

2014-2016 collision map for Ottawa for pedestrians, cyclists and drivers
