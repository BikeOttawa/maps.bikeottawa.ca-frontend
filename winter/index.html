Winter Maintenance


This map shows which pathways in the city of Ottawa are plowed in the winter and which are not.

The map is maintained by Bike Ottawa volunteers.

If you see any mistakes or familiar with the status of a pathway in your neighbourhood, feel free to submit feedback by clicking on the path and writing a quick note.
